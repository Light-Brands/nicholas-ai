## A Seven-Volume Book Series by Nicholas Courchesne

---

> *"Bringing the lost travelers home to the gardens of their souls."*

---

## The Soul Mission

There are souls wandering this earth who have forgotten the way home. They built empires but lost themselves in the building. They achieved everything the world told them to want, yet woke each morning to an emptiness that success could not fill. They smiled for cameras while dying in silence.

These are the lost travelers. And this book series is for them.

**The Internal Transformational Epicenter** documents one man's 45-year journey from fractured performer to integrated wholeness—and provides a roadmap for others to follow. The series is built on a foundational truth: *before you can become a transformational epicenter for others, you must first become one within yourself.*

## Key Frameworks

### The Three Covenants

Central to this story are three unconscious vows that governed Nicholas's life for decades:

| Covenant | Formed | Replaced With |
|----------|--------|---------------|
| "Love equals suffering" | Childhood abuse, ages 8-11 | "Love is safe, present, and healing" |
| "I am my own god" | Departure at 16, self-reliance | "I am a son of the Most High" |
| "Just watch me" | Defiance against doubt | "I surrender to divine guidance" |

### The Trinitarian Self

A key framework revealed through the integration journey:

- **Body (Dark Warrior):** Experiences time and sensation in the physical realm
- **Soul (Light Warrior):** Interprets time, bridges the seen and unseen worlds
- **Spirit (Creator):** Eternal union with the Holy Spirit, untouched by time

**The Divine Order:** Spirit → Soul → Body

## Target Readers

This series is written for:

1. **The Successful but Empty** — Those who have achieved external success while dying internally
2. **The Chronically Recovering** — Those who have done "all the right things" but still feel unhealed
3. **The Spiritually Curious** — Those open to sacred encounter but needing a framework
4. **The Future Healers** — Those called to help others but who must first do their own work
5. **Spouses and Families** — Those loving someone through the transformation journey

By the end of these seven volumes, readers will have:
