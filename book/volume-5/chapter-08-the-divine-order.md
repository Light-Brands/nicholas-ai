# Chapter Eight
## The Divine Order

---

*"Spirit Leads Soul Leads Body"*

---

Understanding the three parts of the self is valuable.

But understanding is not enough.

What matters is *order*.

---

## The Revealed Architecture

During my sacred encounters and the integration that followed, God showed me the divine architecture of healing.

There is a design—a proper order in which the three parts of the self should relate.

**Divine Order:**
Spirit → leads → Soul → governs → Body

Or in my language:
Creator → leads → Light Warrior → governs → Dark Warrior

When this order is operating, everything works. The spirit receives guidance from God, transmits it to the soul, which then directs the body. Alignment. Harmony. Peace.

---

## The Inverted Order

But this is not how I lived for forty-five years.

I lived in inverted order.

**False Order:**
Body → activates → Soul → ignores → Spirit

Or:
Dark Warrior → drives → Light Warrior → suppresses → Creator

In this arrangement, the body's needs and reactions dictate everything. The soul scrambles to manage the body's demands—seeking pleasure, avoiding pain, reacting to threats. And the spirit is ignored, suppressed, or consulted only in emergencies.

This is how most of humanity lives.

The body craves, and we satisfy.
The body fears, and we flee.
The body desires, and we pursue.

The soul interprets and justifies whatever the body demands.

And the spirit waits, silent, in the basement of our being.

---

## The Consequences of Inversion

Living in inverted order produces predictable consequences:

**Addiction:** When the body leads, it will always seek more—more pleasure, more stimulation, more escape. The soul becomes the body's servant, providing rationalizations and strategies for getting what the body wants.

**Anxiety:** When the body leads, its nervous system reactions become primary. The soul cannot override fear with truth because the soul is subordinate to the body.

**Depression:** When the spirit is suppressed, we lose connection to our source of life. The soul, cut off from eternal meaning, collapses under the weight of merely temporal existence.

**Relapse:** Recovery efforts fail because they try to manage at the soul level (willpower, understanding, new behaviors) while the body still leads. The body will eventually override the soul's good intentions.

**Inauthenticity:** When the body leads and the spirit is suppressed, we cannot access our true selves. We perform versions of ourselves based on what the body needs for safety and survival.

Most of my problems—the addiction, the anxiety, the depression, the relational dysfunction—could be traced to inverted order.

---

## Restoring Divine Order

The ibogaine and Bufo experiences did something I couldn't do myself:

They restored divine order.

In the flood dose, as the covenants burned, the false operating system was being uninstalled.

In Bufo, as unity was experienced, the spirit was awakened to its true nature and authority.

In the integration, the new order was being learned and practiced.

Spirit → Soul → Body.

Now, when a situation arises:

1. I first consult my spirit: What does God's peace tell me? What is the eternal perspective?
2. Then I engage my soul: How do I interpret this? What decision aligns with truth?
3. Then I direct my body: What action should my body take?

The body is no longer driving. The body is being directed.

---

## What This Looks Like

**In Craving:**

Old pattern: Body craves → Soul rationalizes → Body satisfies → Guilt
New pattern: Body craves → Spirit noticed → Soul interprets (this is the old pattern, not true need) → Body redirected

The craving doesn't disappear. But it no longer commands. The spirit provides perspective. The soul makes the actual decision. The body follows.

**In Anxiety:**

Old pattern: Body tenses → Soul catastrophizes → Body panics → Spiral
New pattern: Body tenses → Spirit noticed → Soul interprets (this is nervous system, not truth) → Body regulated through breath and presence

The body still reacts. Bodies react. But the reaction doesn't become reality. The spirit holds peace. The soul chooses truth over catastrophe. The body calms.

**In Relationship:**

Old pattern: Body feels threatened → Soul defends → Body attacks or withdraws → Disconnection
New pattern: Body feels threatened → Spirit noticed → Soul interprets (this is old wounding, not actual threat) → Body responds with presence instead of reaction

Relationship becomes possible because the body's defensive reactions don't control behavior anymore.

## The Biblical Foundation

This is not new-age invention. It is biblical anthropology.

*"Walk by the Spirit, and you will not gratify the desires of the flesh."* — Galatians 5:16

*"The spirit is willing, but the flesh is weak."* — Matthew 26:41

*"The mind governed by the flesh is death, but the mind governed by the Spirit is life and peace."* — Romans 8:6

*"May your whole spirit, soul and body be kept blameless."* — 1 Thessalonians 5:23

The Bible consistently presents a view of human nature in which spirit, soul, and body exist in relationship—and in which the Spirit of God is meant to lead.

Divine order is not my invention. It is God's design.

---

## The Practice

Maintaining divine order requires practice.

The body wants to lead. It has been leading for most of our lives. The neural pathways that put body in charge are well-worn. They don't disappear overnight.

Restoring divine order requires:

**Spiritual Practice:**
- Daily time with God that strengthens the spirit
- Prayer that reestablishes spirit-leadership
- Scripture that renews the soul with truth
- Presence practices that quiet the body's demands

**Soul Renewal:**
- Identifying the lies the soul has believed
- Replacing them with truth
- Training the will to follow spirit rather than body
- Processing emotions rather than suppressing them

**Body Regulation:**
- Nervous system practices (breathwork, grounding)
- Physical health that supports regulation
- Somatic awareness (noticing body signals without being controlled by them)
- Rest, nutrition, exercise

The goal is not to suppress the body—it is to put the body in its proper place, following the soul that follows the spirit.

---

## Freedom in Order

Here is the paradox:

When I was body-led, I felt enslaved.

The cravings controlled me. The anxiety controlled me. The reactive patterns controlled me. I was not free—I was a slave to my flesh.

When spirit leads, I am free.

Not because I suppress my body. But because my body is now directed rather than directing. My spirit has access to peace that transcends circumstances. My soul aligns with truth. My body receives clear instruction.

This is what the Bible means when it says *"Where the Spirit of the Lord is, there is freedom."*

Freedom is not absence of structure. Freedom is proper order.

The Creator leading the Light Warrior leading the Dark Warrior.

Spirit leading soul leading body.

This is the freedom I now live.
